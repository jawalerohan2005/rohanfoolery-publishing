Welcome

This page contains additional digital material associated with the physical edition of this book. I hope you enjoy your coloring book Nicole.

Dear Nicole,

We've been through so much and accumulated so many children along the way, so I though I'd try to capture the memory of all of them in a little book (couldn't do the human ones because how am I supposed to draw Antonio and Alisha). I hope this book helps you keep a little whimsy in your life at all times, and becomes something you can look back at for inspiration going forward. Sometimes, you just need to be like Wiggles and sniff some flowers, or explore the mountains like Broccoli. While my drawings aren't perfect, I hope you like them anyways, and that I was able to capture the feeling of what it was like to exchange all of them little ones throughout the holidays we've celebrated so far together. Here's to a life of peace, growth, and most importantly, silliness.
Love,
Rohan

About the Author

Rohan Jawale graduated in 2019 from Silly Goose University with a PhD in Rohanfoolery, a subcategory of Tomfoolery which was created by Tom Goose, son of Silly Goose, who started Silly Goose University.

He went on to start a very decorated career path, becoming a comedian, author, and much more. He was voted Silliest Man Alive from 2023–2025 and Sexiest Polish Man in 2024 despite being Indian.

He now spends his days bringing joy to the world by simply existing.
